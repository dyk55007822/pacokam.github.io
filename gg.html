About Me

Paco Kam is a dedicated clarinetist with extensive experience in performance, teaching, and orchestral work. Based in Hong Kong and currently studying in Sydney, Paco specializes in classical and jazz clarinet repertoire, delivering performances that captivate audiences and set him apart in the world of music.
